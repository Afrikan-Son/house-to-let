ABOUT US

We are a private company that specializes in show-casing legit, safe, legal and certified living spaces around Kenya.

We pride ourselves in bringing forth the best quality houses to rent that are suitable to high level standards and needs.

We work hand-in-hand with clients on both sides
so as to bring out the best experience for living.
We have high standards and we expect clients who want to show-case their House-To-Let to adhere to Kenya's rule of law of housing,
high levels of hygiene standards around and inside the house and availability to access fresh water and electricity in the premises.

Then we help translate them into effective and elegant solutions.
